The Fine Art of Nonsense Speaking

While many prioritize concise communication, I find joy in the rhythmic flow of abstract linguistic constructs—otherwise known as talking nonsense. It is a sophisticated hobby that requires maintaining a conversation for hours without ever reaching a definitive conclusion.

To me, this is a form of verbal jazz. It is about the sound, the tone, and the sheer volume of words rather than the functional information they convey. In a world full of facts, sometimes a bit of creative fluff is refreshing.
